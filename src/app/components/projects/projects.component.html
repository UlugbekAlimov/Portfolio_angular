Quiz Web

This is my first Angular project: Quiz Web. On this website, you can test your knowledge by taking quizzes on various categories. After completing a quiz, you can view the results of your answers. If you want to create your own quiz, go to the /quiz route and feel free to create your own tests.

Book tj (Clone 42.uz)

Book.tj is a platform for reading, designed for convenient access to literature. Here, you can find books across various genres and topics. All materials are available in digital format. The platform offers recommendations, an easy-to-use search, and personalized lists. The goal of Book.tj is to make reading accessible to everyone.
